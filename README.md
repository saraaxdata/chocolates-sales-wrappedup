## üíº Business Problem
The sales team spent 2+ hours onmultiple Excel files to track revenue, products, and regional performance. Reporting required manual consolidation each week, making it slow, error-prone, and reactive.

As a result:
* Sales and profit were visible, but profitability by product was unclear
* High-revenue products were incorrectly assumed to be high-margin
* Regional performance issues surfaced late or not at all
* Identifying top sales performers required manual effort
* Leadership lacked a single, trusted source of truth

Decisions were based on partial insight instead of real-time data.

<br>

## üéØ Why this dashboard
This Power BI dashboard replaces fragmented Excel reporting with a single, interactive analytics layer that delivers instant visibility into sales, profit, people, and markets.

It enables stakeholders to:
* Identify top products and salespeople
* Track profit margins
* Understand regional performance
* Monitor sales targets
